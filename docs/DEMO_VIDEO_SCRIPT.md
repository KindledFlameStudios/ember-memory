## Recording Notes

**What to capture:**
1. **Screen recording:** OBS Studio or similar (1080p, 60fps)
2. **Terminal theme:** Something clean (Catppuccin, Tokyo Night)
3. **Controller UI:** Make sure dashboard is visible and readable
4. **Pacing:** Fast but not rushed — let key moments land

**What NOT to do:**
- No corporate voiceover — use your own voice or text-to-speech
- No flashy transitions — keep it authentic
- No over-explaining — show, don't tell

**Optional additions:**
- Background music: Lo-fi, chill, non-distracting
- Subtitles: Auto-generate for accessibility
- Chapters: Timestamp key sections for YouTube
